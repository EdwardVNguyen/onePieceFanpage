What is One Piece?
With over 516.6 million copies sold and known as of one the greatest manga of all time, One Piece is an award winning series that follows the adventures of Monkey D. Luffy and his pirate crew as they adventure through the vast sea in search for the legendary treasure known as the One Piece.
Why Read One Piece?
Selling over 516.6 million copies world wide, not only making it the best-selling manga series, but also the best selling comic series in volume-format.
One Piece is set in a world populated by humans, fishmen, giants, and many more! One Piece tells a tale of Luffy D. Monkey, who is in search of Gol D. Rogers famous treasure, the One Piece.
As of August 2025, One Piece has 1100 chapters worth of content and it is still ongoing. One Piece's author, Eiichiro Oda has been working on One Piece since 1997! Making One Piece a 28 year long series!
Established in a grand and diverse world, the cast inside One Piece are memorable and endearing. The main cast alongside side characters and villians are given deep stories and motivations, making the world of One Piece feel alive and vibrant.
